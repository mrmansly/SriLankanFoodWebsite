Trained as a software engineer, cooking was always a side passion of mine. It allowed me to relax after a hard days work and switch off from the constant buzz.

When I was living abroad in New Zealand and Melbourne with my wife, Sharnell, I needed my regular fix of rice and curry which could rarely be satisfied through the usual takeout joints. It was during this time that my love of cooking home-style Sri Lankan curries and rice really took off. If I couldn't buy my curry fix, I needed to be the one doing the cooking!

We would regularly entertain our friends by inviting them over for rice and curry for birthday parties, dinner parties and the odd AFL Grand Final party! It was great to see my friends eating curries that I had grown up with, and seeing them coming back for seconds and thirds.

Throughout these early phases of my cooking, there was of course the constant calls back home to ask Mum how much of this ingredient I should put in, or when do you know something is ready, or why isn't this looking the same as yours? As time went on, the questions became less frequent, the curries became more consistent and on the odd occasion you get the ultimate feedback from your guests that it's "Restaurant Quality"!

My goal is to bring fresh home cooked Sri Lankan food that my family and friends have loved to your home!

I want to spread that unique taste that Sri Lankan food has to offer to whet your appetite and get you so hooked on it that you can't go more than a week without getting your curry fix on!

BUT unlike me, you have the opportunity to buy your home cooked Sri Lankan curries rather than having to make it yourself!
