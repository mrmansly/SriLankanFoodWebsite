Welcome to your favourite Sri Lankan website for tasty treats!

My only job is to make your life easier.
I'll provide the food, you go spend some time with your family!

Since I can remember I've lived in a house where Wednesday dinner and Sunday lunch was rice and curry time! Throughout the years I've watched my Mum make our favourite curries over and over again, and seen them devoured just as quickly.

In those days I helped prepare the ingredients, cut the veggies, stirred the pot, and tasted the food.

Now I make the rice and curries for my family - some recipes from my Mum (well most of them actually), but also some others I've picked up along the way and have made it onto my list of favourites. It's nice to know that my daughter, Lola, also enjoys cooking and eating curries just as much as I do.

These days Lola helps prepare the ingredients, cut the veggies, stirs the pot, and tastes the food.

Rice and curries in my house have always been made to share and enjoy with others. Each of my family have their own favourite curry, but it wasn't a meal unless there were at least three or four other curries to go along with a heaped spoon of rice.

I welcome you to peruse through the menu and take a look at the dishes that I've grown to enjoy cooking and enjoying with my family.

I hope if you have a fondness for Sri Lankan curries, or are willing to step out of your comfort zone and venture away from the Indian, Thai or Chinese meals that you'll find something here that you'll be able to share and enjoy with your family too!
